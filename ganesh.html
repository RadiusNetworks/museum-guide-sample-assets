Madonna With Child

Cuzco, Peru 2004

Oil on canvas

This modern oil painting captures the essence of the classic Cuzquena school style that dominated Alta Peru during the colonial era. The elaborate fabric of the mother's gown mirrors the importance of fine fabrics with complex textures to the Incan people. The gold leaf overlay and the feather plumes on the headdress echoes the high status of the painting's subject in a way fitting to a viewer used to seeing such embellishments on Incan leaders.
