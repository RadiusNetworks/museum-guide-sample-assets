Buddha Dragon Boat

Hue, Vietnam 2011

Carved teak

A common traditional theme in Vietnam, elders depicted as stylized Buddha images ride on a whimsical boat in the shape of a dragon. Four men sit around a game table. Another in front guides the ship with a globe in hand. Musicians in the rear entertain the passengers. This fantastic specimen is carved from a single piece of wood. Note the birds perched on the rear of the boat, and the separate base depicted with sea creatures.
